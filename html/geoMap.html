Prohodna Cave

Prohodna is one of the most famous caves in Bulgaria, located in the village of Karlukovo, Lovech region. This is the longest cave tunnel in Bulgaria, with a length of 365 meters and a height of its arches of 56 meters, formed at the end of the Cretaceous period 66 - 68 million years ago.

Krushuna Waterfalls

Krushuna waterfalls are located on the northern slope of the Devetashko plateau in the middle Fore-Balkans. The cascade was formed along the Proinovska River, which springs from the Krushuna Cave. The first and largest waterfall in the cascade is the 15-meter Praskaloto, which together with the next waterfalls, are commonly called Krushuna Falls.

The Stone Forest

The Stone Forest, also known as The Stone Desert, is a desert-like rock phenomenon located on the northwest Varna Province border in Bulgaria. It is considered the only desert in Bulgaria and one of few found in Europe.
